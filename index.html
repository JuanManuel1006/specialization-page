About The Specialization

In this specialization the central theme is art and its different branches and forms of expression, such as drawing, painting, photography and architecture. Here the basic concepts of art are learned and different techniques and ways of making art are explored in a better way. Ways of showing art to the world are also taught, as well as how art influences people's lives, being an important factor in each of these.

Goal of This Specialization

This specialization seeks to give a more artistic approach to students and help them find their vocation, also to take into account that art can influence many aspects of daily life.
